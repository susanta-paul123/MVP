Overview

Audit & Certification Services

Based on TIA-942 Audit & Certification, 3rd party ISO-24762, ISO-27001 and ISO 20000 Audits, Compliance Reviews and Certifications Services. With extensive and rigorous process to assess compliance to the requirements of the standards involves over 3,500 criteria to be assessed throughout Architectural, Electrical, Mechanical, Security, Safety and Telecommunication.

Data Center Design Validation

Focused on helping customers with validating the designs prepared by consultants or suppliers and verification of designs against applicable standards and/or Tier levels. Validation is done before product and solutions orders take place, to ensure everything is correctly designed and specified to match the need and requirement

Data Center Audit & Certification

Certification services provide certification of data centers based on various standards such as ANSI/TIA-942, ISO/IEC-24762, ISO/IEC-20000, ISO/IEC-27000. offering Tier level certification based on compliance reports of intense architectural, electrical, mechanical, maintenance, and documentation audits.

Data Center Assessment

The onsite review includes performing a careful review of key criteria elements such as:

  • Construction
  • Site Location
  • Power Infrastructure
  • Physical Security
  • Electromagnetic Fields
  • Telecom Infrastructure
  • Maintenance Contracts
  • Estimating efforts and resource utilization change requirement
